Earlier today the incoming Nyalenda B MCA joined Nyalenda youths in celebration of their graduation ceremony.

18 September, 2021 by Imelda Olang 884
  • Share on Facebook
  • Tweet on Twitter

Earlier today incoming Nyalenda B MCA, HON SAMMY BOY had to leave all his businesses aside, inorder to join graduants from Nyalenda at Akado training centre, where they were celebrating after successfully completing their various certificate courses. When the leader was asked why he had to leave all his businesses just to create time for youths, and the leader responded, "Youths demonstrate higher career aspirations, increased self-esteem, and improved school completion rates and also They have a greater understanding of the problems facing other youth, and fresh perspectives for how to address these problems when offered they support and the resources they might need" He also acknowledged the fact that he really loves working together with youths in solving matters that oppresses them.

He told the graduants, that was a very important event in there lives, cause it opens doors to other chapters in life, and that meant a different chapter of there lives and the only way to remain relevant is to add value always and make ourselves better people through enlightment and education. The youth leader who has been working around the clock to ensure his community gets the best it deserves, even though despite the fact that he is not part of the current government, he assured his community that he shall continue doing the same in or out side the government cause he knows and understands what it feels like to go through the hood life and will not stop fighting for his community until they get the best that they deserve.

Hon Sammy Boy, finished by reminding youths to vote for leaders who really care about there affairs but not leaders who are looking forward to ascending to power just to loot there resource. He urged them to vote wisely, but not jusy for the sake of who gives most handouts(bribery).
